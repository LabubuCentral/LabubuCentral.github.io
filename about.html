About Labubu Central

Your trusted source for Labubu collectibles and information

Welcome to Labubu Central

Labubu Central is the ultimate destination for Labubu collectors and enthusiasts worldwide. We're passionate about these adorable monster figures created by Hong Kong artist Kasing Lung and brought to life by Pop Mart.

Our Mission

We exist to help collectors find the best Labubu figures at the best prices. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting your Labubu journey, we provide comprehensive information about products, prices, and where to buy authentic figures.

What We Offer

  • Product Information: Detailed descriptions and pricing for all Labubu series
  • Authentic Retailers: Links to verified sellers like Pop Mart, Amazon, Target, and Walmart
  • Expert Guides: How-to articles on collecting, authentication, and care
  • Latest News: Updates on new releases and limited editions
  • Community Focus: Building connections between Labubu collectors

Why Trust Us

Our team consists of dedicated Labubu collectors with years of experience in the collectibles market. We personally verify all retailer links and product information to ensure you're getting authentic figures at fair prices.

Transparency

We believe in full transparency with our community. When you purchase through our affiliate links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us maintain and improve our website while keeping all content free for collectors.

Join Our Community

Follow us on social media and subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on the latest Labubu releases, exclusive deals, and collector tips. We're here to support your collecting journey every step of the way.
