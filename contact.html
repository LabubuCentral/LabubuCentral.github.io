Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell Labubu figures directly?

No, we don't sell figures directly. We're an informational website that helps you find the best places to buy authentic Labubu figures from verified retailers like Pop Mart, Amazon, Target, and Walmart.

How do I know if a Labubu figure is authentic?

We only recommend authorized retailers like Pop Mart, Amazon, Target, and Walmart. Always check for official Pop Mart branding and packaging. We have detailed guides in our blog section to help you authenticate figures.

Can you help me find a specific Labubu figure?

Absolutely! Contact us with details about the figure you're looking for, and we'll help you locate it from our trusted retail partners.

Do you offer price matching?

Since we don't sell directly, we recommend checking multiple retailers for the best prices. Our catalog shows current prices from different sellers to help you compare.
